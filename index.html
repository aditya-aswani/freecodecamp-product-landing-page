How It Works

Pause Pal takes place over the course of 4 weeks. Pairs of pals will be matched based on their preferences as specified in the form. During the four weeks, each pair of pals will meet according to the frequency and length of the meditation sessions they committed to.

  • Fill

    Fill out the above form with your meditation frequency and length preferences.

  • Commit

    Read this doc and commit to the guidelines.

  • Schedule

    Receive and confirm your meditation schedule calendar invites.

  • Meditate

    Meditate together as outlined in the schedule.

  • Extend

    Consider meditating together after the month is over.
